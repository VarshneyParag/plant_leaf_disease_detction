Common Plant Diseases

Our AI can detect a wide range of plant diseases. Here are some of the most common ones we identify:

Powdery Mildew

A fungal disease that appears as white powdery spots on leaves and stems.

  • Common in cucumbers, squash, and roses
  • Causes yellowing and curling of leaves
  • Thrives in humid conditions

Leaf Spot

Characterized by dark spots on leaves, often with yellow halos.

  • Affects many ornamental plants and vegetables
  • Caused by fungi or bacteria
  • Can lead to defoliation if severe

Blight

Rapid and complete chlorosis, browning, then death of plant tissues.

  • Common in potatoes and tomatoes
  • Can destroy entire crops quickly
  • Favored by warm, wet weather

Rust

Appears as rusty-colored pustules on the undersides of leaves.

  • Common in beans, wheat, and roses
  • Weakens plants and reduces yields
  • Spreads through wind and water

Root Rot

A condition where roots decay due to overwatering or fungal infection.

  • Common in houseplants and container gardens
  • Causes wilting and yellowing of leaves
  • Often fatal if not treated early

Bacterial Wilt

Causes sudden wilting and death of plants without yellowing.

  • Affects cucumbers, melons, and squash
  • Spread by cucumber beetles
  • No effective treatment once established
